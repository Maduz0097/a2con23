a2con'23

The coronation ceremony of the young talented leaders and the recognition of the next generation of pioneers.

Leo District 306 A2 is home for more than 1400 youth driving with a vision to make a difference and spearheaded under the theme “Changing Paradigms” for the Leoistic year 2022/2023. With endorsements and acknowledgments from the most influential leaders, Leo District 306 A2 is committed to build a strong cadre of future youth leaders.
Taking forward the illustrious legacy of the former leaders of Leo District 306 A2, we proudly present to you A2 CON 23, the Annual District Conference 2023, the coronation ceremony of the young leaders of Leo District 306 A2.
Having become accustomed to the new normal, the world is now entering a new year. With new hopes, comes the opportunity and the responsibility of creating a world where young people can find their place in. The Annual District Conference intends the youth to be recharged, with better energy and momentum and recognize the efforts and their dedication towards the betterment of Leoism.

  • Vision
  • Mission

To bring forth a new dimension for the recognition of the potential youth leadership of the country.

To promote awareness, and the recognition of the force of talent and skills of the youth in one platform and together building a team of young professionals paving way for the voice of youth volunteers to be heard worldwide

Leo District 306 A2

Leo District 306 A2 is one of the leading Leo Districts in Sri Lanka. It is sponsored by the Lions Clubs International, District 306 A2 and presently 25 Leo clubs are actively serving the community with the contribution of more than 950 Leos under the guidance of multi-talented executive officers. Activities conducted by Leo District 306 A2 include Childcare, Eldercare, Sports, Environmental, Healthcare, Leadership Development Projects and many more and over 1000 projects are conducted annually with a beneficiary count of over 100,000 individuals.
